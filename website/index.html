The Challenge

Non-profits often operate with limited visibility into donor lifecycle and campaign effectiveness. This comprehensive analysis bridges that gap by identifying high-value periods, donor retention patterns, and optimized engagement triggers.

Key Findings

  • Retention is Key: Repeat donors provide the stable foundation for long-term sustainability.
  • Cyclical trends: Peak donation periods align with specific campaign triggers and seasonal behaviors.
  • Campaign Impact: The Animal Welfare campaign shows the highest engagement.
  • High-Value Windows: Financial spikes correlate with targeted outreach efficiency.
